Noita Cauldron Forecast

NOTE: The cauldron's contents are determined based on the date when you first load its area each game, and again each NG+ cycle.
The date is based on UTC time, which is probably different from your local time.

WARNING: In December of 2024 (and future Leap Years), contents will be selected at random each time the area is loaded due to a likely bug.
Contents will still be either Void Liquid or Air, but will differ within the same day.
